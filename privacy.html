Privacy Policy

At Prescriptive, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. We understand the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of your data and are committed to providing a safe and secure platform for all your medication management needs. If you have any concerns or questions about our privacy practices, please reach out to us at prescriptiveapp@gmail.com.

At Prescriptive, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect from you, how we use it, and the measures we take to keep it secure.

Information we collect from you

When you use our app or website, we may collect personal information such as your name, address, email address, and phone number. We collect this information for the purpose of providing you with our services, including ordering medication, comparing prices, refilling prescriptions, and connecting with doctors. We may also collect information about your health conditions and medical history to ensure the accuracy and safety of our services.

Access to your Personal Information

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information at any time. If you would like to do so, please contact us at privacy@prescriptive.com.

How we use your information

We use your personal information to provide you with our services and improve your medication management experience. This includes processing your orders, comparing prices, refilling prescriptions, and facilitating communication with doctors. We may also use your information to send you important updates and notifications related to your medication and health.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information

In addition to personal information, we may collect non-personally identifiable information about your usage of our app and website. This includes data such as your device type, browser type, IP address, and browsing behavior. We use this information to analyze trends, improve our services, and enhance your user experience.

Storage and Security of your information

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented industry-standard measures to protect it. Your information is stored on secure servers and access is restricted to authorized personnel only. We regularly review our security practices to ensure the ongoing safety of your data.

Cookies and Pixels

We use cookies and pixels on our app and website to enhance your browsing experience and personalize our services. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device and allow us to remember your preferences and provide customized content. Pixels are small images that allow us to track user activity and gather anonymous data for analytics purposes. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.
